We are a small business salon in King William's Town, this Beauty Salon started in 2018 in a small setting and it grew big as the time went by. Over the years we were able to secure our own place so that it can be easier for customers to find the location. When the Covid-19 hit, the buisness sufferd a loss because of lack of interraction.We decided to introduce a new system of booking an appointment online so that we can limit the number of people inside the salon. This system helped the business a lot, even when the covid-19 ended we kept on reciving bookings online which grew the buisness even more

Mission

At Bola's Beauty Salon, our mission is to provide exceptional beauty services in a warm, welcoming and relaxing enviroment.We strive to empower our clients to feel confident and beautiful while fostering long-term relationships built on trust, quality and personalized care.

Vision

Our vision is to become the top beauty salon in King William's Town,known for our exceptional service, talented team and commitment to customer satisfation.We aim to create a loyal community of clients who trust and recommend us to others.
